The Overview tab is built for speed. Open a chat and you immediately see the headline stats and the patterns that matter.

## What it includes

- Total messages and participants
- Quick insights like most active time
- A distribution view so you can see who is sending what

![Overview dashboard](/features/overview-dashboard/combined-01-overview+02-overview-scrolled.webp)

If you only have time to check one screen, this is the one.
